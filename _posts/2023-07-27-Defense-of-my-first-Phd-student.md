Eduardo Meca Castro, a PhD candidate from the Electrical and Computer Engineering department from FEUP (School of Engineering, University of Porto), has successfully defended his thesis today. 
The jury was unanimous in its decision. Jury members: Ana Mendonca (FEUP), Joao Manuel Tavares (FEUP), Ana Barata (IST), Luis Alexandre (UBI). 

Luis Almeida (FEUP) on behalf of the department (ECE) serving as president, and myslef as Eduardo's supervisor, were also part of the jury.

UPDATE: Eduardo received the honor of _Cum Laude_.
